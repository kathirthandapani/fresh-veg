Roasted Heritage Carrots
with Fennel Pollen

Curated by Chef Julian Solis. A celebration of earth and sweetness, highlighting the natural sugars of winter roots.

+4k
have cooked this
schedule Prep

15 min

oven_gen Cook

45 min

equalizer Level

Medium

The Story

A visually stunning side dish that brings out the natural sugars of heritage carrots. The Purple Haze carrots from Blue Hill Farm retain a vibrant violet hue when roasted, creating a beautiful contrast with the bright orange interior. The fennel pollen adds a whisper of licorice that pairs perfectly with the artisan honey glaze, elevating a simple root vegetable into a centerpiece.

Preparation

Prepare the Carrots

Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Scrub the carrots clean (peeling is optional for heritage varieties). Halve the larger carrots lengthwise.

The Glaze

In a small bowl, whisk together the artisan honey, olive oil, and a pinch of sea salt. Toss the carrots in this mixture until evenly coated.

Roast

Arrange on a baking sheet. Roast for 35-45 minutes, turning once halfway through, until tender and caramelized at the edges.

Finish

Transfer to a serving platter. Immediately sprinkle with the fennel pollen and fresh fennel fronds while hot. Serve immediately.
